Conformal Prediction Meets Test-Time Uncertainty in Vision Foundation Models
Huaze Liu, Adyasha Mohanty
[Milestone Report]

We applied split Conformal Prediction for uncertainty quantification in vision foundation models, demonstrating reliable statistical coverage for open-vocabulary semantic segmentation. Currently, we are extending our framework with DINOv3, stress-tested diffusion-based perturbations, temporal smoothing, and end-to-end evaluation with confidence-aware planning for Jetson Orin powered drones.
